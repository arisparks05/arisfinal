SOPHMORE OIL WORKS by me Ariana Sparks

The works that will be displayed are pieces created during my oil painting class this semester. Oil painting is a new medium for me, and this exhibition serves as both a showcase of my progress and a personal reflection of my learning experience. Throughout the semester, I explored various techniques, learned to manipulate textures, and embraced the richness of color that oil paint offers. This collection of pieces not only marks my journey into the world of oil painting but also captures my development as an artist. The specific pieces presented are the ones I enjoyed working on the most, as they reflect moments of experimentation, discovery, and growth.
